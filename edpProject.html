How To Win

The objective of the game is to eliminate or capture the flag of the opponent, or to maneuver one's flag to the far edge of the board. More specifically:

  • If you eliminate the opposing flag using any piece (including your own flag)
  • If you maneuver your flag to the opposite side of the board and survivng a turn without being challenged or without any adjacent opposing pieces
  • If your opponent chooses to resign due to being cornered or a lack of possible winning moves
